# Project Management Template

## Project Management Structure & Guidance

This template provides a structure for:
- Project phases and milestones
- Key questions for planning and execution
- General PM best practices

### Example Project Phases
- Initiation
- Planning
- Execution
- Monitoring & Control
- Closure

### Key Questions
- What is the project objective?
- Who are the key stakeholders?
- What are the major risks?
- What are the critical success factors?
- What is the timeline and budget?
- What are the deliverables?
- How will progress be tracked?

### Best Practices
- Maintain clear documentation
- Communicate regularly with stakeholders
- Review risks and update mitigation plans
- Track progress against milestones
- Document lessons learned at closure
